About

Treasure Garden Investment Ltd is a leading Real Estate Company registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, with unparalleled expertise in several subsets of Real Estate including Construction, Development, Rentals, Structural Designs, Architectural Designs, Road Construction, Survey, Property Brokerage, and Real Estate Portfolio Management. Our unwavering dedication to honesty and the provision of services in an effective and value-driven manner is shown by the unique stamp of excellence which trail our services to our rich array of clients who trust us to seamlessly and professionally meet their Real Estate needs.

Mission

As a company, we are driven by core principles which represent our corporate mission, and as client-centric enterprise, the thrust of our mission revolves around providing services of exquisite and resounding quality for our clients. Our mission include:

  • To make the buying and selling of Real Estate as cost effective as possible while maintaining the highest level of service.
  • To provide accurate and up-to-date information, skilled analysis, and sound Real Estate advice.
  • To continually explore new ideas and technology to make the buying and selling of Real Estate faster, cheaper, and easier.
  • To facilitate the construction and development of world-class housing infrastructure in Nigeria.

Vision

Our vision is to be rallying point for Real Estate construction, development, sale, and other ancillary services in Africa's largest economy, Nigeria. We aim to change the Real Estate trajectory in Nigeria by leading the charge with acclaimed global best practices which are reflective of the peculiar needs of our clients, and the uniqueness of the Nigerian environment.
